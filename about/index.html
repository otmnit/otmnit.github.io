About

Hey, I’m otmnit — a Network Engineer with a Master’s degree in Networking and IoT, completed in Paris, France.

I created this media to share my CCNP journey, insights from my learning, real-world situations I encounter at work, and the latest news from the networking and tech industry.

I aim to stay authentic in everything I publish. I sometimes use AI as a tool to help me correct grammar or structure my ideas more clearly, but never to generate topics or decide what I write about. It’s a support tool, not a replacement for thinking.

This project is something I’m building for the long term — with the goal of becoming a reference in the networking space over the years.
